My Experience

This is what I have done

Professionally, I started working at Crumbl Cookies in December 2021 and stayed through June 2022. Since it was a new store, I had the opportunity to learn every role and become part of building a strong team culture from the ground up. I helped train new employees, maintained a clean and safe work environment, and made sure customers had a great experience. It taught me how to work efficiently in a fast-paced setting and communicate clearly with both coworkers and guests.

From July 2022 through July 2024, I served as a full-time missionary, where I developed leadership, public speaking, and teaching skills. I facilitated district councils, mentored new missionaries, and connected with up to 25 new individuals each week. I later served as a zone and district leader from June to October 2023 and again from February to June 2024. In those roles, I worked closely with mission leadership to plan, lead trainings, and help drive a 1600% increase in baptisms by focusing on intentional outreach and consistent follow-up. I also compiled reports and helped mentor new zone leaders.

Giving back has always been important to me. Since 2020, I’ve volunteered over 50 hours at local food banks. Between 2021 and 2024, I also dedicated over 100 hours to various service projects—helping families with moves, organizing a charity run to support individuals with multiple sclerosis, and leading a community landscaping project. These experiences have helped me stay grounded and reminded me how meaningful it is to serve and support others.
